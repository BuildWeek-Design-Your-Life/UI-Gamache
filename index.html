Mission Statement

We have become so busy, that most of our days go by in a blur. Many of us take part in activities without being mindful as to how they affect us and make us feel. Do we even enjoy our "fun" activites?

Here at 'Design Your Life', we want to help you figure out what actually brings you joy. We want to help measure what truly engages you and fulfills your desires. Our site gives you the opportunity to quickly and easily record your activitites as well as how they make you feel. Armed with this information, you will be able to plan activities for the future that will bring you enjoyment and fulfillment.

WE ARE HERE TO REDESIGN YOUR LIFE.

Services

  • The opportunity to log activities in a quick and easy way.
  • You will be able to measure how engaging your activity is.
  • You will be able to measure how enjoyable each activity is.
  • You will have the opportunity to reflect on your entries.
